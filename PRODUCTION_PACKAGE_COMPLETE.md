## ðŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

1. **Start with the easiest scenes first**
   - Interrogation room (simple geometry)
   - Title card (just train model)
   - Build confidence before complex scenes

2. **Reuse everything possible**
   - Same lighting rig for multiple scenes
   - Same camera animation, different scene
   - Modular props used in multiple shots

3. **Render overnight, work during day**
   - Queue renders before bed
   - Work on modeling/animation during day
   - Never waste time watching render bars

4. **Test in Eevee, final in Cycles**
   - Eevee for all tests (instant)
   - Only use Cycles for hero shots
   - Saves hundreds of hours

5. **One act at a time**
   - Complete Act 1 fully before starting Act 2
   - Feels accomplishment faster
   - Can publish in parts if needed

6. **Motion graphics can be simple**
   - Clean > flashy
   - Information > animation
   - Fern's graphics are minimalist
   - Don't overcomplicate

7. **Sound design sells it**
   - Great visuals + bad audio = amateur
   - Good visuals + great audio = professional
   - Spend time on sound

8. **Color grade makes everything cohesive**
   - Even mismatched renders look unified
   - With good grade
   - Save for last
   - Transform the piece
